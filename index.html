How to Play

Checkers is a two-player game where the goal is to capture all of the opponent's pieces or block them so they cannot move.

Players take turns moving their pieces diagonally forward. If a player can jump over an opponent's piece, they capture it. There are no double captures in this game so keep that in mind.

The game ends when one player captures all of the opponent's pieces or blocks them so they cannot move.
